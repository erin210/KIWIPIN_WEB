・FAQ

  • What is the difference between Kiwi Pin and Kiwi Wallet?
    ➜ Kiwi Pin:
    1. A one-time prepaid points card containing a top-up code (pincode) and card serial number, redeemable for services on specific platforms.
    2. Can be used without creating an account; simply enter the pincode after purchase to redeem the service.
    ➜ Kiwi Wallet:
    1. A digital points wallet for storing and managing your Kiwi Pin credits.
    2. Supports accumulating points and making direct payments, ideal for frequent users.
    3. Provides advanced features, such as transaction history review and balance top-ups.
  • What are the unique features of Kiwi Pin and Kiwi Wallet?
    ➜ Kiwi Pin:
    1. Single-purchase and instantly usable.
    2. No account registration required — simply enter the top-up code (pincode) to redeem.
    3. Ideal for gifting or temporary purchase needs.
    ➜ Kiwi Wallet:
    1. Centralize the management of point assets to prevent loss of KiwiPIN points.
    2. Supports the accumulation of multiple top-ups and remaining balances, allowing flexible usage of points.
    3. Offer transparent transaction history for users to easily monitor their activity.
  • What situations is Kiwi Pin suitable for use?

    When you need to quickly purchase points or only require a one-time use of a platform’s service, Kiwi Pin is the most convenient option because it does not require account binding. After purchase, entering the top-up code (pincode) activates the points immediately. For example, you can buy a set of Kiwi Pin top-up codes (pincodes) and directly redeem them into your game account.

  • Can Kiwi Wallet generate Kiwi Pin codes?

    No, Kiwi Wallet cannot directly generate Kiwi Pin top-up codes. Users need to use the top-up function within the wallet to purchase additional Kiwi Pin credits, or buy them via the official website or authorized resellers.

  • What is the difference in validity periods between Kiwi Pin and Kiwi Wallet?
    ➜ Kiwi Pin:

    Valid for one year. Users must redeem the points within the specified timeframe.

    ➜ Kiwi Wallet:

    Once credits are loaded into the wallet, they can continue to accumulate according to platform rules without a specific expiry date.

  • If I purchase a Kiwi Pin, do I have to register for Kiwi Wallet?

    No. The Kiwi Pin pincode can be redeemed directly on supported platforms without requiring a Kiwi Wallet.

・Fraud Prevention

  • What are some common types of scams?
    1. Using fake websites to lure users into purchasing point cards at extremely low prices.
    2. Scammers posing as customer service representatives to request point card serial numbers or payment receipts.
    3. They claim there is a problem requiring a refund, but ask for bank details or additional payments.
  • What should I do if I receive a potentially fraudulent message?

    Do not click on any suspicious links or provide your personal information. Please contact customer service immediately at Contact Customer Service (service@kiwipin.com) and provide details of the suspicious message or content. We will assist you in handling the issue.

  • What should I pay attention to when purchasing a point card?

    Please make sure the point card you buy fits your needs, such as platform or regional restrictions.

  • Is it possible to return or get a refund?

    Point cards are digital products. Once sent, they cannot be returned or refunded. Please confirm before purchasing.

・Points

  • How do I purchase a KIWI point card?
    1. Go to the official website’s Purchase Points page and select the desired card denomination.
    2. Enter your recipient email address.
    3. Select a payment method and complete the payment.
    4. The top-up code (pincode) will be automatically sent to the email address you provided.
  • What payment methods are supported?

    We offer a variety of payment methods, including credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, JCB), WeChat, and Alipay.

  • How long does it take to receive the top-up code (pincode) after purchase?

    After completing your payment, the top-up code (pincode) is usually sent to your email within a few minutes. If there is a delay, please contact customer service (service@kiwipin.com) for assistance.

  • What if I purchase the wrong point card?

    Point cards are digital products and cannot be exchanged or refunded once sent. If you purchase the wrong card, you may top it up into your Kiwi Wallet. Please be sure to confirm the type and amount before purchasing.

  • What should I do if I don’t receive the top-up code (pincode)?
    1. First check your spam or junk mail folder to see if it was misclassified.
    2. Confirm that the payment was successfully completed and verify that the correct email address was entered.
    3. If the issue is still not resolved, please Contact Customer Service (service@kiwipin.com) and provide your order number for assistance.

・Wallet

  • What is a Kiwi Wallet?

    The point wallet is a digital tool that allows you to directly top up on various partner platforms. It enables you to centrally manage your purchased Kiwi points, making it easy and quick to check your balance and transaction history.

  • How do I create a Kiwi Wallet account?
    1. Click the "KIWI Wallet" button on the official website navigation bar, or go to (KIWI Wallet URL), and fill in the basic information (such as email, password, etc.).
    2. After verifying your email, your point wallet account will be successfully activated.
  • Is it possible to transfer the points in the wallet to other users?

    No. Currently, the Kiwi Wallet does not support point transfers. Points can only be used by the account holder and cannot be transferred to other users or accounts.

  • What should I do if I forget my account or password?
    1. Click the "Forgot Password?" link on the login page.
    2. Enter the email you used during registration, and the system will send you instructions to reset your password.
  • What if I forget my transaction password?

    After logging in, click on "Transaction Settings" and select "Forgot Password?". The system will automatically send a new transaction password to your registered email.

  • Does the Kiwi Wallet support multiple currencies?

    Transactions are denominated in USD, but purchases can be made in multiple currencies.

  • How do I recharge my Kiwi Wallet?
    1. Click the "+" button and select the desired top-up amount and denomination.
    2. Choose your payment method (such as credit card, e-wallet, or Kiwi Pin code). Once the payment is completed, the points will be immediately credited to your wallet.
  • How can I view my wallet transaction history?

    Click on "Transaction History" in the left navigation menu to view detailed information on all top-ups and payments.

  • Can I get a refund for unused points in my wallet?

    Once the points have been recharged or redeemed to the wallet, they cannot be refunded. However, if there is an issue such as a transaction error, please contact customer service (service@kiwipin.com) for assistance.

  • Can I withdraw unused points as cash?

    The wallet balance is only for use within the supported platforms and cannot currently be withdrawn as cash.
